Upon completion of the program the participant must demonstrate the following core competencies:

  • Keyboarding
    Minimum 25 words per minute (WPM)
  • Operating Systems
    Basic level of proficiency with Windows OS
  • Graphic Design Skills
    Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Color Theory, Fundamentals of Typography, Design and Composition, Graphic Design, Fundamentals of Logo Design, Print Production
  • Presentations
    Intermediate level of proficiency in Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Word Processing and Spreadsheets
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel
  • Office Skills
    Personal Computer Fundamentals, basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Outlook and Internet Fundamentals
  • Business Skills
    Customer Service, Marketing and Sales, Project Management Fundamentals, Design Business
