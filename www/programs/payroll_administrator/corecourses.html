Upon completion of the program the participant must demonstrate the following core competencies:

  • Keyboarding
    Minimum 25 words per minute (WPM).
  • Operating Systems
    Basic level of proficiency in a Windows operating system
  • Word Processing
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Word
  • Spreadsheets
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Excel
  • Office Skills
    Personal Computer Fundamentals for End Users, Basic Bookkeeping Level 1, and basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Outlook
  • Accounting
    Payroll Compliance Legislation, Payroll Fundamentals Levels 1 and 2, and QuickBooks Premier
  • Simulations and Drills
    Practical Applications – 1 Unit
  • Business Skills
    Business Math, Business Correspondence Level 1, and Customer Service
  • Job Readiness/Employability Skills
    Job Search and Résumé Writing and Thought Patterns for a Successful Career
