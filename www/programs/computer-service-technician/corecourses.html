Upon completion of the program the participant must demonstrate the following core competencies:

  • Keyboarding
    Minimum 25 words per minute (WPM).
  • Operating Systems
    Advanced level of proficiency in a Windows operating system
  • Word Processing
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Word
  • Spreadsheets
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Excel
  • Data Management
    Basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Access
  • Office Skills
    Internet Fundamentals and basic level of proficiency in Microsoft Outlook
  • Business Skills
    Grammar Essentials for Business Writing, Customer Service, and Workplace Success/Intrapreneurship
  • Job Readiness/Employability Skills
    Job Search and Résumé Writing and Thought Patterns for a Successful Career
  • PC Troubleshooting
    CompTIA A+ 220-1001 and CompTIA A+ 220-1002
  • Computer Networking
    CompTIA Network+ and CompTIA Security+
