After submitting many resumes, I realized that I was unable to meet the requirements of most employers. My years of experience no longer mattered. I needed to upgrade my skills to be marketable and competitive in today’s workforce. I decided on Academy of Learning Career College. The Facilitators were tremendous and always ready and able to help. I liked the idea of self-paced learning. I was able to complete some of the subjects quickly, and others were more challenging and required extra time and effort. In less than a year I graduated with an Honours diploma. I had a sense of accomplishment and felt very pleased with myself." ALEXIS ADAMS - Past Graduate

Academy of Learning gave me the courage to keep going and complete my goal of becoming a bookkeeper/accounting graduate. Thank you for being my cheer leading section when I felt like I didn’t have one!
